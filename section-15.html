    Section 10.5 Derivatives of Trig Functions

    Exercises

    Derivatives.

    Compute the derivative of each of the following curves at the given point.
    1.
    \(y=x\sin(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}=\left( x\cos(x) + \sin(x)\right)\)
    2.
    \(y=x\cos(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}=\left( \cos(x)-x\sin(x)\right)\)
    3.
    \(y=x^2\cos(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}=\left( x(2\cos(x)-x\sin(x))\right)\)
    4.
    \(y=2\sin(x)\cos(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}= 2\cos(2x)\)
    5.
    \(y=\cos(x)\tan(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}= \cos(x) \)
    6.
    \(y=x^2\sin(x)\cos(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}=\left( x^2\cos(2x)+x\sin(2x)\right)\)
    7.
    \(y=x^2\tan(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}= x^2\sec^2(x)+2x\tan(x)\)
    8.
    \(y=\cos^2(x)-\sin^2(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}= -2\sin(2x)\)
    9.
    \(y=\cos^2(x)+\sin^2(x)\)
    Answer.
    \(\dfdx{y}{x}= 0\)

    Derivatives at a Point.

    Compute the derivative of each of the following functions at the given point:
    10.
    \(y= \dfrac{1}{2+\sin(x)}\text{,}\) \(x=0\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle \left.\dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=0} = \eval{\frac{-\cos(x)}{(2+\sin(x))^2}}{x}{0} = -1/2\)
    11.
    \(y= \dfrac{1}{2+\sin(x)}\text{,}\) \(x=\pi/2\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle \left.\dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=\pi/2}= \eval{\frac{-\cos(x)}{(2+\sin(x))^2}}{x}{\frac{\pi}{2}} = 0 \)
    12.
    \(y= \tan(x)\text{,}\) \(x=\pi/4\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=\pi/4} = \eval{\sec^2(x)}{x}{\pi/4}= 2\)
    13.
    \(y= \cot(x)\text{,}\) \(x=\pi/4\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=\pi/4} = \eval{-\csc^2(x)}{x}{\pi/4} = -2\)
    14.
    \(y= \sin(x)(1+\cos(x))\text{,}\) \(x=\pi/3\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=\pi/3} = \eval{\left(-\sin^2(x)+(1+\cos(x))\cos(x)\right)}{x}{\pi/3} = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)
    15.
    \(y= \sin(x)(1+\cos(x))\text{,}\) \(x=-\pi/3\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=-\pi/3} = -3+\sqrt{3}\)
    16.
    \(y= x\sin(x)+\cos(x)\text{,}\) \(x=0\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=0} = 0\)
    17.
    \(y= x\sin(x)+\cos(x)\text{,}\) \(x=\pi\)
    Answer.
    \(\displaystyle\left. \dfdx{y}{x}\right|_{x=\pi} = -\pi\)
    18.
    \(\displaystyle y=\frac{\sin(x)\cos(x)}{\tan^2(x)}\)
    \(x=\frac{\pi}{2}\)
    Answer.
    \(\eval{\dfdx{y}{x}}{x}{\frac{\pi}{2}} = \eval{3\cot^2(x)\csc^2(x)}{x}{\frac{\pi}{2}}=0\)
