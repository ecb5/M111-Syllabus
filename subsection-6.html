    Subsection 11.2 Making Things "Easier on the Eyes"

    Exercises

    Exercise Group.

    Find \(\dx{y}\) for each of the following.
    1.
    \(y= (2x+3)^6\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y}=12(x+3)^5)\dx{x}\)
    2.
    \(y= (7-3x)^7\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y}=-21(7-3x)^6\dx{x}\)
    3.
    \(y= (7-3x)^{-7}\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y}=21(7-3x)^{-8}\dx{x}\)
    4.
    \(y= \left(\frac{x}{2}+\frac{2}{x}\right)^3\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y}=3\left(\frac{x}{2}+\frac{2}{x}\right)^2\left(\frac12-\frac{2}{x^2}\right)\dx{x}\)
    5.
    \(\displaystyle y=(x^3-1)^2\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = 6x^5-6x^2\dx{x}\)
    6.
    \(\displaystyle y=x\sqrt[3]{1+x^2}\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = \dfrac{\frac53x^2+1}{\sqrt[3]{(1+x^2)^2}}\dx{x}\)
    7.
    \(\displaystyle y=(2x-3)^4(x^2+x+1)^5\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = (2x-3)^3(x^2+x+1)^4[5(2x-3)(2x+1)+8(x^2+x+1)]\dx{x}\)
    8.
    \(\displaystyle y=\left(\frac{x^3-1}{x^3+1}\right)^8\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} =\left( \dfrac{48x^2}{(x^3+1)^9}\right)\dx{x}\)
    9.
    \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{2x+\sqrt{5x-\sqrt{7x}}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2x+\sqrt{5x-\sqrt{7x}}}} \left( 2+ \frac{5+\sqrt{\frac{7}{x}}}{2\sqrt{5x-\sqrt{7x}}} \right)\dx{x}\)
    10.
    \(\displaystyle y=[(x^2+3)^3(1-x^3)^2]^5\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = 5\left[(x^2+3)^3(1-x^3)^2\right]^4\left[6x(1-x^3)(x^2+3)^2(1-3x-x^3)\right]\dx{x}\)
    11.
    \(\displaystyle y=-\left(\left(-x^2\right)^2-x\right)-\left(\left(-x\right)^3+x\right)^3\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = \left[2x-1-3(-x^3+x)^2(-3x^2+1)\right]\dx{x}\)

    Exercise Group.

    Find the pattern.
    12.
    (a)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^2(x-1)^2\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = 2x(x-1)(2x-1)\dx{x}\)
    (b)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^3(x-1)^3\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = 3x^2(x-1)^2(2x-1)\dx{x}\)
    (c)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^4(x-1)^4\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = 4x^3(x-1)^3(2x-1)\dx{x}\)
    (d)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^a(x-1)^a\) where \(a\) is an unspecified constant.
    13.
    Find the pattern.
    (a)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^2(x-1)^3\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = x(x-1)^2(5x-2)\dx{x}\)
    (b)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^3(x-1)^2\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = x^2(x-1)(5x-3)\dx{x}\)
    (c)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^4(x-1)^7\)
    Answer.
    \(\dx{y} = x^3(x-1)^6(11x-4)\dx{x}\)
    (d)
    \(\displaystyle y=x^a(x-1)^b\) where \(a\) and \(b\) are unspecified constants.
