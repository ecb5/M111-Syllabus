    Subsection 5.2 Quizzes

    Week 1.

    The first week is a bit problematic since we only meet twice a week. We’ll discuss how we will handle the first quiz on the first day of class.
    There will be a quiz at the end of class every Wednesday, except when exams are scheduled. Each quiz will be:
    • \(20\) minutes long,
    • worth \(10\) (quiz) points.
    The average of all of the quizzes will contribute 150 (course) points toward your final course grade. There will be no makeup quizzes for any reason because the lowest quiz score will be dropped.
