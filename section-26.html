    Section 11.15 L'Hopital's Rule

    Exercises Exercises

    Exercise Group.

    Use L'Hopital's Rule to compute each of the following limits:
    1.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\cos(x) -1}{x}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\cos(x) -1}{x}}= 0\)
    2.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{x}{\cos(x)+1}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{x}{\cos(x)+1}}= =\)
    3.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\sin(\alpha x)}{\sin(\beta x)}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\sin(\alpha x)}{\sin(\beta x)}}= \dfrac\alpha\beta\)
    4.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\tan(\alpha x)}{\tan(\beta x)}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\tan(\alpha x)}{\tan(\beta x)}}= \dfrac\alpha\beta\)
    5.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{e^x-1-x}{x^2}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{e^x-1-x}{x^2}}= \dfrac12\)
    6.
    \(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x}{\ln(1+2e^x)}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x}{\ln(1+2e^x)}}=1 \)
    7.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\cos(mx)-\cos(nx)}{x^2}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{\cos(mx)-\cos(nx)}{x^2}}= \dfrac{n^2-m^2}{2}\)
    8.
    \(\limit{x}{1}{\frac{x^a-ax+a-1}{(x-1)^2}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{1}{\frac{x^a-ax+a-1}{(x-1)^2}}= \frac{a(a-1)}{2}\)
    9.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{1-e^{-2x}}{\sec(x)}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{1-e^{-2x}}{\sec(x)}}= 2\)
    10.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{1-e^{-2x}}{\sin(x)}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{1-e^{-2x}}{\sin(x)}}=2\)
    11.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{(1-e^{-2x})\csc(x)}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{(1-e^{-2x})\csc(x)}=2\)
    12.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{x\ln(x)}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{x\ln(x)}=-\infty\)
    13.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{(\ln(x))}{\frac1x}}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\frac{(\ln(x))}{\frac1x}}=-\infty\)
    14.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\cot(2x)\sin(6x)}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\cot(2x)\sin(6x)}=3\)
    15.
    \(\limit{x}{\infty}{x\tan(1/x)}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{\infty}{x\tan(1/x)}=1\)
    16.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\sin(x)\ln(x)}\)
    Answer.
    \(\limit{x}{0}{\sin(x)\ln(x)}=0\)
