    Section 10.18 Areas and Volumes

    Exercises Exercises

    Regions Between Curves:.

    Find the areas of the regions enclosed by the graphs of the given equations:
    1.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 4x+4\\ y \amp = 6x+6\\ x \amp = 4 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(25\)
    2.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = \cos(x)\\ y \amp = \sin(x)\\ x \amp = \frac{\pi}{4}\\ x \amp = 5\pi/4 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(2\sqrt{2}\)
    3.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = e^x\\ y \amp = e^{2x}\\ x \amp = \ln(4) \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac92\)
    4.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 6x\\ y \amp = 3x^2-6x \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(32\)
    5.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 9x\\ y \amp = x^3 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{81}{2}\)
    6.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = \abs{x-3}\\ y \amp = \frac{x}{2} \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(13\)
    7.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 2-\abs{x}\\ y \amp = x^2 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac73\)
    8.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = \sin(x)\\ y \amp = \sin(2x)\\ x \amp = 0\\ x \amp = \pi \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac52\)
    9.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} x \amp = \abs{y}\\ x \amp = 2-y^2 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac73\)
    10.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 8x\\ y \amp = 9-x^2\\ y \amp = \frac52x \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{17}{3}\)
    11.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = 2x^2\\ y \amp = 4\sqrt{2x}\\ y \amp = -4x+6 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{13}{6}\)

    Solids of Revolution.

    Find the volume of the solid resulting when the area bounded by the graphs of the following equations is revolved about the \(x\)-axis.
    28.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp =e^{-x}\\ y \amp =0\\ x \amp =0\\ x \amp =\ln(4) \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{15\pi}{32}\)
    29.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp =\frac{1}{\sqrt[4]{1-x^2}}\\ y \amp =0\\ x \amp =0\\ x \amp =\frac12 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{\pi^2}{6}\)
    30.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = \sqrt[4]{x}\\ y \amp = x \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{\pi}{3}\)
    31.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = x\\ y \amp = 2\sqrt{x} \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{32\pi}{3}\)
    32.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = x\\ y \amp = x+2\\ x \amp = 0\\ x \amp = 4 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(48\pi\)
    33.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+x^2}}\\ y \amp = 0\\ x \amp = 0\\ x \amp = 1 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{\pi^2}{4}\)
    34.
    \begin{alignat*}{1} y \amp = x^2-6x+10\\ y \amp = -x^2+4x+2 \end{alignat*}
    Solution.
    \(\frac{2597\pi}{48}\)
