It is extremely unwise to wait until the night a set of WeBWorK problems is due to begin doing them. This is because it will not be clear how to do some of the problems the first time you look at them. If you don’t start on them until the night they are due you will not have a chance to ask about them in class, so you will not learn what you need to learn from those problems.

WeBWorK advice.

Your best option is to open and try the WeBWorK problems as soon as they open. Some of them will be challenging and you don’t want to be doing them in a panic at the last minute.
This will be reflected in your exam grades and your final course grade.
