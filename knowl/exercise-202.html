Exercise 20. Unusual Pairs of Functions.

Show that the following pairs of functions differ by a constant value regardless of the value of \(x\text{.}\) In each case, find the constant.
(a)
\(f(x)= \inverse\sin\left(\frac{x-1}{x+1}\right)\) and \(g(x)=2\inverse\tan\left(\sqrt{x}\right)\text{.}\)
Hint.
(b)
\(f(x)= \inverse\sin\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}}\right)\) and \(g(x)=\inverse\tan\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)\text{.}\)
