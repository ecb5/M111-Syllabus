Example 7.0.1. Participation Score.

For example: Suppose this class is conducted \(12\) times. Then \(C=12\text{.}\) If you earned \(2\) points (this is the most you can earn) in \(10\) of them, and only \(1\) in the other two then \(P=2\times 10 + 1\times 2=22\text{.}\)
In that case your participation score is \(225\times\frac{P}{C}=412.5\text{.}\)
