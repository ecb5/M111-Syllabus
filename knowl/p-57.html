I do not use the gradebook on Moodle. If something shows up there it is because I couldn't find a way to prevent it. I keep grade records offline in a spreadsheet. Moreover, I do not keep your grade records in a form that allows me to tell you what your "current" grade is. If you want a running record of your "current" grade you have to keep your own records of your Quiz, WeBWorK, Participation, and Exam scores.
