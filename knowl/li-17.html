If I Don’t Respond to an Email

If you send me an email and I do not respond it does not mean that I hate you, or that I’m ignoring you. It simply means that I didn’t see it.
If I see your email I will make every possible effort to respond. If I don’t respond in a reasonable time please send it again. Or, if there is no rush, bring your the issue to me during the next class. I’d rather talk to you in person anyway.
